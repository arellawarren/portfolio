ABCDerium

The ABCDerium is an art book inspired by abecedaria, or the inscription of alphabetical letters. The purpose of this particular book was to pair 26 distinct typefaces with complementary photographs in a visual exploration of color, light, and form.

In addition to designing the book and capturing all of the photographs myself, I printed and bound a physical copy, which was put on public display at the Kohler Art Library in Madison, WI.
